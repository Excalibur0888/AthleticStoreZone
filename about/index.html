About AthleticStoreZone

Your trusted partner for premium sports equipment in India since 2010

Our Story

AthleticStoreZone was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide athletes across India with access to high-quality sports equipment at fair prices. What started as a small store in Mumbai has grown into one of India's leading sports retailers, with multiple locations across the country and a robust online presence.

Our founder, Rajiv Sharma, was a former national-level cricket player who understood the importance of quality equipment in athletic performance. Frustrated by the lack of reliable sports gear in the market, he decided to create a store that would cater to athletes of all levels â€“ from beginners to professionals.

Today, AthleticStoreZone continues to uphold its founding principles of quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction. We partner directly with leading global brands to ensure that every product we sell meets our rigorous standards.
