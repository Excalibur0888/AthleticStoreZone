How do I track my order?

You can track your order in real-time through the app. Simply go to the "Orders" section and select your order to view detailed tracking information, including estimated delivery time and current status.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways, and we never store your complete card details on our servers.
